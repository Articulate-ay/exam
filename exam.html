ABOUT US

Established in 2008, Pansula was a buy-and-sell, family-owned business that strictly operated in fabric and workwear. As the business grew, we decided to start manufacturing our workwear. We paved our way in the competitive market and built a solid customer base throughout Africa as we are able to provide the finest garments combined with the best customer satisfaction. This has helped us to establish a deep foot print in the informal sector as wholesalers, retailers, distributors and consumers.
